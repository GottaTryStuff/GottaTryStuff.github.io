    Quick Search

    com.unity.quicksearch

    Description

    Search multiple areas of Unity: assets, scene objects, menu items, packages, APIs, settings, etc.

    On Windows simply press Alt+’ to find anything or Option+’ on Mac.
    Look in the shortcut manager for additional Quick Search shortcut bindings.
